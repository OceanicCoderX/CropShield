Tomato Leaf Spot Disease
Replied
Crop Name
Tomato
Crop Part
Leaves
Query Date
28 October, 2025
Symptoms
Brown circular spots appearing on tomato leaves with yellow halos. Spots are increasing in size and affecting multiple leaves. The plant appears weak and growth has slowed down.
Uploaded Images
Admin Response
Question
Have you noticed any water droplets or high humidity around the affected leaves? Are the spots appearing on lower leaves first?
Answer & Diagnosis
Based on the images and symptoms, this appears to be Early Blight (Alternaria solani), a fungal disease common in tomatoes. The brown circular spots with concentric rings are characteristic of this disease. It thrives in warm, humid conditions.
Suggested Product
🌿 Fungicide - Mancozeb 75% WP
Effective against Early Blight. Apply 2.5g per liter of water. Spray every 10-14 days. Remove affected leaves and improve air circulation.

Rice Blast Disease
Replied
Crop Name
Rice
Crop Part
Panicles
Query Date
20 October, 2025
Symptoms
Gray-white lesions on rice panicles with dark borders. Grains are not filling properly and appear shriveled. Yield loss is significant.
Uploaded Images
Admin Response
Question
When did you first notice these symptoms? Have you been maintaining proper water management in your field?
Answer & Diagnosis
This is Rice Blast (Pyricularia oryzae), a serious fungal disease affecting rice panicles. The disease causes significant yield loss if not managed properly. It spreads rapidly in cool, wet conditions.
Suggested Product
🌿 Fungicide - Tricyclazole 75% WP
Highly effective against Rice Blast. Apply 1g per liter of water. Spray at boot stage and again at 50% flowering. Maintain proper field drainage.
